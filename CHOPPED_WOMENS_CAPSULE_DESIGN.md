## 1. UNDERSTANDING SUMMARY

* **Product Concept:** The **CHOPPED. Women’s Midnight Core Capsule**—a 4-piece high-performance streetwear lineup designed for women and night-shift veterans of the grind.
* **Fit Profile:** A dual-profile collection combining **Boxy & Heavyweight Crop / Oversized** upper garments with **Sleek & Form-Fitting** tapered fleece bottoms.
* **Decoration Method:** **Hybrid Execution**—minimalist high-density chest/sleeve embroidery on the front + technical DTG graphic/text prints on the back.
* **Color Palette & Vibe:** Deep Asphalt (`#080808`), Vintage Black, Charcoal (`#2A2A2A`), and Industrial Silver/Off-White accents.
* **Explicit Non-Goals:** Bright multi-colored lifestyle apparel, thin promo garments, or full sublimation all-over prints.

---

## 2. ASSUMPTIONS & CONSTRAINTS

* All garments selected are sourced from Printful's active catalog with support for DTG printing and embroidery fulfillment.
* Fabric weights prioritize mid-to-heavyweight feel (100% combed/ring-spun cotton or heavy cotton/poly fleece).
* Black backgrounds in artwork must be 100% transparent PNGs to prevent DTG white box halos on dark fabric tones.

## 5. TECHNICAL PRODUCTION FILE & QUALITY CONTROL RULES

1. **Direct-to-Garment (DTG) Artwork:**
   * 300 DPI transparent PNG files in sRGB color space.
   * Backgrounds must be 100% transparent.
   * Keep print elements at least 1.5" away from seams, pocket borders, and raw crop hems.

2. **Embroidery Digitization:**
   * Maximum 12,000–15,000 stitches per placement to avoid fabric puckering on cropped lightweight fabrics.
   * Minimum text height: 5mm (0.2 in).
   * Thread spec: Madeira 1800 Series (Matte Charcoal `#2A2A2A`, Off-White `#F4F4F4`, Deep Asphalt `#121212`).
