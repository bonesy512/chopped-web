## 5. Key Limitations & Design Considerations

> [!WARNING]
> ### Things Printful Cannot Do That CHOPPED. Specs Require
> - **Reflective / crack-ink printing** — DTG and DTFilm are standard ink processes. No reflective or specialty metallic inks. Use silver/grey tonal substitutes.
> - **Internal structural features** — Lumbar-Lock™ liner, hidden orthotics, internal knee pads — these are custom garment features outside POD capability.
> - **Custom hardware** — Antique brass slides, matte steel buckles, industrial zippers. Printful uses standard garment hardware.
> - **Hand-distressing** — The Otto Cap 104-1018 is pre-distressed, but not "hand-abraded" to spec. Close enough for POD.
> - **Fabric weight** — Heaviest tee is ~220GSM (vs. 300GSM spec). Heaviest hoodie is ~440GSM (vs. 500GSM spec). Close but not exact.
> - **Wool blend beanies** — Printful beanies are primarily acrylic. No true wool-blend options.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> ### Recommendation for Launch Copy
> Per [brand-voice-guidelines.md §7](../brand/brand-voice-guidelines.md), scarcity is a **timed ritual**, not a stock claim. For POD products, adjust product descriptions to emphasize the design/graphic elements and brand identity rather than specific GSM weights or custom hardware that the Printful blanks won't match. Save the exact technical specs for the future made-to-order premium line.
